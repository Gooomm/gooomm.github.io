SP MOMIJI

The stunning spirit of the Red Forest. When she first set eyes on Seimei, it changed her life forever. The depth of her love for him is matched only by Shuten Doji's love for her. Both, however, are ultimately unrequited. Perhaps all love is nothing but tragedy.He was dressed in black, and he spoke softly to me. Ah! His voice was so sweet. I wanted to melt in it, to let it surround me. He said, "Leave nothing. Devour everything." I followed his command. How could I refuse? Since becoming a ghost, I've fed on every single mortal and spirit that has laid eyes on me. They feed my beauty. I grow more wondrous by the day. Even the rancid smell of decay is bearable, thanks to him. I endure it and I never stop feeding, not even for a moment. Now, no matter what I eat, I can only taste flesh. And because of this, nobody is more beautiful than I.
